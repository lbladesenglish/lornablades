My Story

I was on the path to a career in marketing in London, a field that combined my love for creativity and communication. During a backpacking trip to Canada everything changed. I met people from around the world and realised how much I enjoyed helping them communicate across languages and cultures.

That experience led me to English teaching and eventually to Spain where I began working with corporate students.

When the world moved online during the pandemic I found my niche working one-to-one with professionals to help them communicate clearly and confidently in English.
