About Lorna

Lorna Blades is a communication and business English coach who helps professionals express ideas clearly and confidently in workplace situations. She has worked with leaders, sales teams and technical experts across global companies, combining structured methodology with intuitive coaching to drive measurable progress. Her practical and encouraging approach focuses on clarity, impact and authentic self-expression.

My Story

I was on the path to a career in marketing in London, a field that combined my love for creativity and communication. But during a backpacking trip to Canada, everything changed. I met people from all over the world and discovered how much I loved helping them connect across languages and cultures.

That experience sparked a new direction. I shifted my focus to English teaching and moved to Spain, where I began working with corporate students. Teaching professionals felt like a natural extension of my interests in business communication and cultural etiquette.

Since then, I have taught in Canada, New Zealand, and Spain, gaining a deep appreciation for how culture shapes communication. When the world went online during the pandemic, I found my niche, working one on one with professionals to help them communicate clearly, confidently, and authentically in English.
